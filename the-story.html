How we met

One fateful Sunday during fellowship at HolyGhost Bar Prayer Network, I saw an angel. I have seen angels in my life but this one was different, I felt it.

HOW HE ASKED?

I wanted to meet her at the end of the meeting but she disappeared before the meeting was over. It happened again and again. Thrice. I would be praying with my eyes closed and she would disappear.

After she disappeared the third time, I learnt my lesson. So, when she came, I prayed with my eyes open. I WATCHED and prayed. Then, I was able to meet her.

The next week she came again but this time I had found out who she came with. And so, I collected her number from her friend. She came again and stayed till the end and we prayed for her.

From that day, I started contacting her and I knew the lord was saying something. Lol

"No." She declined. It felt like a part of me had died. I felt sad but I encouraged my self to keep being friends with her. I wouldn't let such an angel pass me by, just like that.

I didn't give up. I continued checking up on her and her family, and sometimes I would chip in that I really liked her. She would say 'thank you'. Just, thank you. I told her I loved her personality, she said thank you. I kept collecting "thank yous" until one day, I told her I loved her again. "I Love you too" she responded. When the call dropped ehh, I was so happy. I felt like I won a hundred billion lottery. I couldn't eat. I felt bloated with so much joy. This virtuous lady said she loves me too. By April, school stuff came and she had to battle with the decision of staying or traveling for her education, but as God had it , she stayed back.

We came back together because God saw how broken I was when she had left. Right now, I can't wait to say "I do" to her. I really am grateful for covid lockdown because if not, she would have been outside the country, studying. Thank you for being a part of my existence. You compliment me in every way.

I love you "My Woman!"

Pastor Chukwuemeka Obianyo & Min. Faith Nkiruka
